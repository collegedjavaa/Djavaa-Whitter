Art

I enjoy drawing in my spare time. Most times I draw original art and on occassion I draw fanart from mass media. I upload all of my art to my Instagram which is @maubunnnnn (I would have chosen maubunn but it was taken sadly). I came up with the name maubunn because I love the color purple, and mauve is a pale shade of purple; hence 'mau-'. The '-bunn' comes from my love of bunnies as well as bread buns.

I plan on creating my own Webcomic in the future and I plan to host it on Naver's Webtoon site/app!
